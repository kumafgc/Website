Who We Are

About Merseyside FGC

The Merseyside Fighting Game Community (MFGC) is a group of passionate fighting game players who gather to play, compete, and build friendships in the centre of Liverpool. We welcome players of all skill levels and provide a supportive environment for everyone to improve and have fun.
